
A MetaTrader 5 breakout Expert Advisor supporting two selectable setups: a Bollinger Bands breakout (strong-body candle closing outside the bands) or a "Mother-Inside-Outside" price-action pattern (inside bar followed by an outside/momentum candle). Momentum candles are filtered by body-to-range percentage and average body size vs. a coefficient threshold. Once triggered, it draws breakout level lines and enters on a stop-style break of the high/low, then supports up to 8 sequential martingale-style re-entry steps after a stop-loss hit, each with its own configurable risk/reward ratio and lot size (fixed lots or % of balance). Includes optional trading-hour restrictions and an on-chart status comment showing live candle/setup diagnostics.
